About Me

I’m Swaroop Kumar Yadav, a driven and curious Electronics and Communication Engineering student currently in my 3rd year. With a passion for innovation and problem-solving, I enjoy exploring the intersection of hardware and software to create impactful solutions. My journey in engineering has been fueled by a desire to understand how technology can transform everyday life.

Whether it’s designing low-power CMOS circuits, developing efficient traffic control systems, or working on RFID-based security applications, I thrive on turning ideas into functional prototypes. Beyond technical expertise, I bring creativity and attention to detail to every project I undertake.

Outside of the classroom, I am deeply interested in the use of digital media in contemporary engineering processes. I believe that communication and visualization are key to bridging the gap between technical ideas and practical implementation. This drives me not only to create innovative projects but also to present them in a way that fosters collaboration and understanding.

I’m always eager to learn, grow, and contribute to meaningful projects that push the boundaries of what’s possible. If you’re interested in collaborating or discussing opportunities, feel free to reach out—I’d love to connect!
